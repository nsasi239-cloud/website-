Instagram

Instagram[a] is an American photo and short-form video sharing social networking service owned by Meta Platforms. It allows users to upload media that can be edited with filters, be organized by hashtags, and be associated with a location via geographical tagging. Posts can be shared publicly or with preapproved followers.

Company Details

Instagram is a major American photo and video-sharing social networking service launched in October 2010 by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger. Acquired by Facebook (now Meta Platforms, Inc.) in 2012, the platform is headquartered in Menlo Park, California, and has grown to over two billion monthly active users as of 2022. Encyclopedia Britannica Key details about Instagram include: Parent Company: Meta Platforms, Inc. Head of Instagram: Adam Mosseri Founders: Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger Key Features: Photo sharing, Stories, Reels, Direct Messaging (DM), and Shopping. Platform Availability: iOS, Android, Windows, and Web. Primary Revenue Model: Digital advertising. Instagram focuses on visual content, allowing users to apply filters and use hashtags to share media publicly or with followers. It has evolved into a key platform for digital marketing, influencers, and brand engagement.
